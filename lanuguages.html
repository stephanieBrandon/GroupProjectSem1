Fun Fact

Did you know that there is a term for people who is gifted at learning multiple languages? Well there is! It is called a hyperpolyglot, people with this possess a particular neurological trait that's well-suited for learning languages very quickly.

Reasons to Learn a Language

While not everyone may be gifted with hyperpolyglot, there are so many reasons to learn another language. Firstly, it's a way to challenge your brain. Secondly, you may find yourself interacting with people you never would have previously, whether from lack of a common dialect or the chance to build your network at a language class.

Benfits

There are many benefits to of learning additional languages. In fact, people who speak more then one language have improved memory, problem-solving and critical-thinking skills.They also are found to be able to concentrate better along side multi-tasking and the abitility to listen.

Cost

It doesn't have to be expensive to learn a new language. There are many great resources on the interent know that make learning any language you desire relativley accesible. It is more about you dedication level and the time you put into it.
